<objective>
Build the production token-usage ledger (D-08/D-09/D-10): a new `token_usage_ledger` table,
a feature-tag plumbed through `setHeadlessUsageSink`, and a best-effort sink wired into the
REAL sleep pass (not the EVAL-04 scratch-DB harness) that appends one row per `claude -p`
call. This is the transparency half of the phase: "this period you spent N tokens, M on
readable docs," broken down by lever so each readout line maps to a toggle.

Cost currency is TOKENS (subscription-billed); the `claude -p --output-format json` envelope
already reports per-call `usage` + `total_cost_usd`, which the sink persists verbatim — retail-$
is the stored translation, tokens are primary.

Purpose: the ledger that 44-05's GET /usage route and the 44-06 panel read.
Output: new table, feature-tagged usage, live sink. Independent of the settings loader (44-01).
</objective>

</decision_coverage>
