<objective>
Lay the model/DDL foundation for derived insights: widen the `node.type` and `edge.kind`
CHECK constraints, add the `node_insight` staleness sidecar, extend the eviction sweep's
FK-safe child-wipe to cover the new sidecar, and add the config knobs (confidence ceiling,
mass/hysteresis/freshness thresholds, dark-default surfacing flag). This is the contract
layer that plans 38-02 (reflector) and 38-03 (recall surfacing) build on — it ships first
so both downstream plans receive a stable type/store/config surface, no codebase scavenger
hunt required.

Purpose: insight nodes cannot be written or evicted until the CHECK constraints widen and
the sidecar's FK child-wipe lands; downstream work would throw `SQLITE_CONSTRAINT` without it.
Output: schema v13 migrations, extended types, sidecar store primitives, eviction child-wipe,
config knobs.
</objective>
